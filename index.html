Privacy Policy

Pasha Pasha

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how "Pasha Pasha" (hereinafter "the App") handles personal information. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Access

The App requires access to your device's camera to provide photo-taking functionality. Photos taken with the camera are processed only on your device and are not transmitted to external servers.

2.2 Microphone Access

The App may access your device's microphone when recording video content. Audio data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers.

2.3 Photo Library Access

The App accesses your photo library to allow you to select existing photos for use in your creations, and to save completed slideshows and collage images to your device. Photos and videos are processed locally and are not uploaded to external servers.

2.4 Advertising & Tracking

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework, the App will request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising purposes. You may choose to allow or deny this request at any time through your device's Settings.

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for the following purposes:

  • Taking, selecting, and saving photos
  • Creating and saving slideshow videos and collage images
  • Providing sharing functionality to social media and other apps
  • Displaying advertisements through Google AdMob

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal information to third parties. However, the App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Used for ad delivery. AdMob may collect device information and advertising identifiers for ad personalization and measurement. For details, please see the Google Privacy Policy.
  • SKAdNetwork: Apple's SKAdNetwork is used for privacy-preserving ad attribution measurement. This allows advertisers to measure campaign performance without revealing individual user data.

5. Data Protection

All content created in the App (photos, videos, collages) is stored locally on your device. The App does not upload, synchronize, or transmit your content to any external servers. We do not maintain any user accounts or cloud storage.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Your Choices

  • Tracking Permission: You can control whether the App is allowed to track your activity for personalized advertising via the ATT prompt or through your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking.
  • Camera & Photo Access: You can revoke camera, microphone, or photo library permissions at any time through your device's Settings.

8. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. If changes are made, we will notify you by updating this page with the new effective date.
